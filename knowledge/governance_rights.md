# COMPREHENSIVE WORKER RIGHTS (INTERNATIONAL & MIDDLE EAST)

## 1. PASSPORT & DOCUMENTS
- **PASSPORT POSSESSION:** In most countries (including Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar), it is ILLEGAL for an employer or sponsor (Kafeel) to confiscate your passport. You have the right to keep your original passport at all times.
- **IQAMA / ID CARD:** Your employer is responsible for providing and paying for your residency permit (Iqama). If they don't provide it, you are at risk of arrest, but it's the employer's legal obligation.

## 2. SALARY & PAYMENTS
- **ON-TIME PAYMENT:** Salary MUST be paid into your bank account (WPS - Wage Protection System) every month. 
- **NO DEDUCTIONS:** Employers cannot deduct money for "Visa Costs", "Work Permit", or "Insurance". These are the employer's costs by law.
- **OVERTIME:** Any work beyond 8 hours a day (or 48 hours a week) MUST be paid at overtime rates (usually 1.5x).

## 3. CONTRACTS
- **LANGUAGE:** You should have a copy of your contract in a language you understand (Bangla or English).
- **JOB DESCRIPTION:** You cannot be forced to do work that is not in your contract (e.g., if you are hired as an Electrician, they cannot force you to be a domestic worker).

## 4. HEALTH & SAFETY
- **PPE:** Employers must provide safety shoes, helmets, and gloves for free if the work is dangerous.
- **MEDICAL:** Employers must provide health insurance and pay for work-related injuries.
- **REST:** You are entitled to at least 1 day off per week and mandatory rest periods during heat (Mid-day work bans in Gulf countries).

## 5. EXIT & RETURN
- **EXIT PERMIT:** In many countries (like Qatar), you no longer need employer permission to leave the country. Check the current local law.
- **EMERGENCY LEAVE:** If a close family member dies, you have the legal right to take unpaid or paid leave to return home.

# WARNINGS AGAINST FRAUD
- Never pay more than the government-fixed migration cost.
- Never pay money to an agent without a proper receipt.
- "Visit Visa" is NOT a "Work Visa". You cannot legally work on a visit visa.
- If an agent asks for money via bKash/Nagad without a face-to-face meeting at an RL-listed office, it is a SCAM.
