# RECRUITING AGENCY GOVERNANCE
All agencies in Bangladesh must be registered with BMET (Bureau of Manpower, Employment and Training).
You can verify an agency RL number at: http://www.old.bmet.gov.bd/BMET/ra_list

# WORKER RIGHTS (INTERNATIONAL)
- Right to hold your own passport.
- Right to receive full monthly salary without unauthorized deductions.
- Right to a safe work environment and protective gear (PPE).
- Right to medical leave and emergency return for family deaths.
- Right to legal support if the contract is breached.
